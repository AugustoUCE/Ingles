One interesting thing about Ecuador is that in a short distance, you can experience different climates. For example, you can be in a cold place in the mountains, and just two or three hours later, you can be in a warm and humid forest. For this reason, today we want to present two amazing places near Quito that many people don’t visit very often, but they are perfect for tourism and short trips.

Mindo

Important Facts

Mindo is part of a cloud forest ecosystem. This means it is a humid forest with a lot of fog and rain. It is one of the most biodiverse places in Ecuador. There are more than 500 species of birds, including hummingbirds and toucans. Because of this, Mindo is very popular for birdwatching.

Recommendations

You don’t need a reservation to visit Mindo, but you should go early. You should bring a raincoat, comfortable shoes, insect repellent, and a camera. We recommend staying one or two days to enjoy all the activities.

Pasochoa

Location

Our second secret spot is Pasochoa. It is located near Amaguaña, about 45 minutes to one hour from Quito. You can go by car or bus, so it is a good option for a short trip.

Activities

In Pasochoa, people can go hiking, take photos, and observe nature. There are different trails, and you can walk through the forest and enjoy the fresh air. It is a quiet and peaceful place.

Opinion

I think Pasochoa is special because it protects a native Andean forest. It is a good place to relax, disconnect from the city, and enjoy nature.
